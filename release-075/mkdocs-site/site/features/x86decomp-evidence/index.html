EvidenceStore.verify_claim

Apply the strict verification gate and persist the resulting state.

VERIFIED requires all of the following: - at least three evidence records; - at least three independent groups; - at least two evidence kinds; - no contradiction records; - every file-backed evidence hash still matches.

This is a governance rule, not a mathematical guarantee that a claim is true.
