x86decomp.pdb

Bounded MSF 7.0 / PDB inventory and PE identity correlation.

This parser intentionally inventories stable container, PDB, TPI/IPI, and DBI metadata. It does not claim complete CodeView type/symbol reconstruction. Every read is bounds checked, stream blocks may be discontiguous, and unsupported variants fail explicitly.
